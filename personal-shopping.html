book your personal shopping appointment today!

Jim’s proudly offers Personal Shopping Appointments. With a Personal Shopping Appointment you will have the full undivided attention of 1 or 2 of our Senior Sales Staff and Tailor, if necessary. If you have shopped with Jim’s in the past, we can use your previous transaction history to pre-select some outfits that will fit and suit your style or occasion. Most appointments are booked during regular business hours which are currently, Monday – Saturday, 9:30-5:30. We would also like to offer Early Shopping Appointments to anyone who would feel more comfortable shopping before the store is open to the public. If you cannot make it in during those times, please reach out and we will do our best to accommodate something that will work.
